Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction drama film directed by Christopher Nolan, who co-wrote the
screenplay with his brother Jonathan. It stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain,
Bill Irwin, Ellen Burstyn, Matt Damon, and Michael Caine. Set in a dystopian future where Earth is
suffering from catastrophic blight and famine, the film follows a group of astronauts who travel
through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humankind.
